Online Voting System:

The Online Voting System is a web-based application that allows voters to cast their votes electronically through the internet in a secure and efficient manner. It ensures proper authentication, maintains voter privacy, and eliminates the need for physical gatherings, making the process faster and more reliable. This project supports the vision of Digital India by promoting paperless and contactless governance, while also addressing challenges such as long queues, manual errors, and limited accessibility in traditional voting. By integrating technology into the electoral process, it aims to make voting more transparent, convenient, and accessible to all.

Face Recognition Attendance System:

The Face Recognition Based Attendance System is designed to automate the process of marking attendance using facial recognition technology. It captures and analyzes facial features of individuals through a camera, matches them with the stored database, and automatically updates attendance records. This system eliminates manual errors, prevents proxy attendance, and saves time compared to traditional methods like roll calls or biometric punching. It offers a fast, secure, and user-friendly solution suitable for schools, colleges, and workplaces.
